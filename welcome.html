Welcome to St. John the Evangelist Parish!

Thank you for your interest in St. John's parish. Whether you are just visiting us for a brief time, looking for a new parish home, are returning to the practice of your Catholic faith, or are interested in finding out more about the Catholic Church, we are happy to have you here.

A parish is a community of believers and a spiritual family that is supportive and loving. It can be a place to grow spiritually, put your faith in action, and use our gifts of time and talent as disciples of Christ. We look forward to answering any questions you may have. Please know that you can connect with us on Facebook or App or contact us by phone or email.

St. John's is truly a place where all are counted as part of your faith family. Again, welcome; I look forward to meeting you.

Sincerely yours in Christ,
Fr. Dominic Toan Tran, Pastor
