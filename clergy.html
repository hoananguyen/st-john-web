Rev. Dominic Toan Tran, Pastor

Fr. Dominic Toan Tran, was born in Ho Nai, Bien Hoa, Vietnam.

He entered St. John Vianney College Seminary in Miami, graduating with a Bachelor’s Degree in Philosophy, and concluded his theological studies at St. Vincent de Paul Regional Seminary, with a Master’s Degree in Divinity in 2011.

Fr. Dominic was ordained to the Priesthood by Bishop Gerald M. Barbarito on May 07, 2011. His first assignment as a newly ordained priest was at St. Juliana Catholic Church and School in West Palm Beach.

While serving as Parochial Vicar at St. Joan of Arc Catholic Church and School in Boca Raton, he continued to study and received a Master of Science specializing in Ecclesial Administration and Management from the Catholic University of America on October 31, 2020.

Bishop Gerald M. Barbarito appointed him as parochial administrator to St. John the Evangelist Catholic Church in Boca Raton in 2020. He was officially installed as pastor in 2021.

He is a Third Degree Knight of Columbus. He served as Spiritual Advisor for the Council of Catholic Women for Central Deanery. He serves as a member of the Priestly Life Committee of the Diocese of Palm Beach, and an adjunct Spiritual Director at St. John Vianney College Seminary in Miami, Florida. Fr. Dominic has a great devotion to the Most Blessed Sacrament of the Eucharist and Our Blessed Mother, Mary
